About Me

My name is Xavi Prats Paredes, I was born in December 2002, so I'm 22 years old (I do the math so you don't have to). I'm from Terrassa, a city near Barcelona, Spain. I kinda look like the guy in the photo. I have a degree in Video Game Design and Development from CITM (Centre de la Imatge i la Tecnologia Multimedia).

I've always loved playing video games, but there's only one thing I love just as much: creating them. In the industry of game development, I've found something that allows me to fuse my creativity, my knowledge, and my technical skills into something I can be proud about.

Nowadays, I'm still constantly learning and deepening my knowledge in game design, whether by analyzing existing games or by creating my own. I consider myself a versatile designer, with strong communication skills and capable of working within the limitations that always exist in game development. My main focus lies in systems design, combat design, and ability design (although it's fair to say that the latter two could often be considered part of systems design as well).
