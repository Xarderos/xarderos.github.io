Mind's Resonance

| Unity | Github | C# |

A game jam-winning action game made with Unity that explores how the echoes of bullying can affect the mind. Players use a simple yet symbolic parry mechanic to reflect these "echoes" back at enemies that are manifestations of internalized trauma.

I worked as a narrative, gameplay and combat designer, contributed to support programming, and designed the main level of the game. It was my first game jam, and after an intense team effort, we were proud to win the Best Game award.
